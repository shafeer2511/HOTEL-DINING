1. Japanese Cuisine

  • Ramen: Rich and flavorful noodle soups with various toppings such as pork, chicken, and vegetables.
  • Tempura: Lightly battered and deep-fried vegetables or seafood, served with a dipping sauce.

2. Chinese Cuisine

  • Dim Sum: Steamed buns and dumplings filled with a variety of meats and vegetables.

3. Tibetan Cuisine

  • Momos: Steamed or fried dumplings filled with vegetables or meat, often served with a spicy dipping sauce.
  • Thukpa: A hearty noodle soup with vegetables and your choice of meat.
  • Shapta: Spicy stir-fried meat (often beef) with onions, tomatoes, and a blend of Tibetan spices.

4. Other Favorites

  • Fried Rice: A classic dish with vegetables, egg, and your choice of meat or seafood.
  • Spring Rolls: Crispy rolls filled with vegetables or meat, often served with a sweet and sour dipping sauce.

  • Shapta (Vegetarian): Spicy stir-fried vegetables with a blend of Tibetan spices.
